CPVC (Chlorinated Polyvinyl Chloride) and UPVC (Unplasticized Polyvinyl Chloride) are both types of PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) materials used for piping systems. However, there are significant differences between the two:

1. Chemical Composition: The primary difference between CPVC and UPVC is their chemical composition. CPVC is derived from PVC through a chlorination process, resulting in a material which can withstand higher temperatures and pressures than UPVC.
